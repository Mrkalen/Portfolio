Heres some information about me.

  • The start of a journey

    After graduating from California State University Fullerton with a bachelors degree in lighting design, I embarked on one of the greatest journeys of my life. In March 2012 I started working for
    The Happiest Place On Earth!

  • Castles, Dragons, and Smiles

    At Disney I was privileged enough to work on some of the most amazing shows with the newest technologies alongside the most talented people in the world. I was permitted the opportunity to design, program, and work with proprietary software and technology all while creating magic for thousands of guests every day.

  • One door closes, another opens

    In 2020 Disneyland was closed due to covid. I was furloughed that March and with every passing week I saw the signs that I wouldn't be going back any time soon. That is when I started exploring other options and discovered my love for coding.

  • Full Stack Developer

    After studying HTML and CSS on my own I decided to take the next step and invest in my future. I attended the LearningFuze bootcamp where I learned all the necessary technologies to become a Full Stack Web Developer. Now, here I am today:
